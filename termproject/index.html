The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. Our owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The Salt Lake City Temple which was finished in 1893 and was the 6th temple to be built by the Church Of Jesus Christ Of Latter Day Saints. The Salt Lake City took 40 long years to complete into what it is today. This Temple is among one of the favorites among Utah residents to be sealed for time and all eternity.

The Logan City Temple which was finished in 1884 and was the 4th temple to be built by the Church Of Jesus Christ Of Latter Day Saints. The Logan City temple took 7 years to complete into what it is today. The Logan temple has a vast history with many stories that will leave you wanting to make the trip back every year.

The St. George Temple which was finished in 1877 and was the 3rd temple to be built by the Church Of Jesus Christ Of Latter Day Saints. The St. George temple took roughly 6 years to complete into what it is today. The St. George temple was organized by President Young as the need for a temple was very great at the time.
